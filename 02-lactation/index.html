Lactating Mothers

Lactation increases energy and fluid needs; maternal diet supports milk quality and recovery.

Subtopics

Nutrition During Lactation

  • Approx. +500 kcal/day energy needs.
  • Emphasize calcium and hydration.

Breastfeeding Support

  • Responsive feeding; correct positioning.
  • Continue breastfeeding up to 2 years and beyond.

Maternal Self-Care

  • Adequate rest and stress reduction.
  • Avoid alcohol; limit caffeine.

Early Obesity Risk Behaviours

BehaviourWhy it matters
High-sugar 'energy foods'Drive excess calorie intake and weight gain.
Unplanned frequent snackingDisplaces nutrient-dense meals.
Sedentary postpartum lifestyleSlows recovery and metabolic health.

Key Messages

Breastfeeding protects mother and child.Balanced diet = healthy milk.Hydrate well.
